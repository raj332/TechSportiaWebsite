About The Event

TechSportia is the annual techfest of Department of ICT,VNSGU.

As the name suggests TechSportia combines "Tech" and "Sportia"."Tech" for "Technical" and "Sportia" for "Sports".

In TechSportia students will get a chance to showcase their technical as well as sports skills by competing in different events and games. Each year TechSportia comes with new themes.

This year TechSportia brings "Harry Potter" theme. All the events will be based on this theme.
